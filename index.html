About

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the University of South Carolina, focusing on advancing the field of Automated Planning through the integration of deep learning and reinforcement learning techniques. My research aims to develop more efficient and adaptable planning systems that can handle complex real-world scenarios.

My research interests span across:

  • Reasoning in Automated Planning
  • Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning
  • Knowledge Representation using Ontologies
  • Large Language Models for Planning
